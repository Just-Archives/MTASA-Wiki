HU/Sounds

These are the sound ids you can use with playSoundFrontEnd.

    • 0 - 20 Selection sounds
    • 27 - 30 Bullet sounds
    • 32 - 33 Selection sounds
    • 34 Radio static
    • 35 Stop Radio static
    • 37 - 38 Tick
    • 40 Selection sounds
    • 41 - 42 Tick (no ammo)
    • 43 - 45 Race countdown
    • 46 Repair
    • 47 White noise static
    • 48 Stop White noise static
    • 49 Static short
    • 101 Countdown/selection

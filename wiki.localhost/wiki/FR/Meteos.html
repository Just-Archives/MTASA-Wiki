FR/Meteos

From Multi Theft Auto: Wiki
Jump to:navigation, search

Weather can be changed using setWeather and setWeatherBlended. Mixed weather ID's between 20 and 255 are also supported.

Weather IDs

  • 0 to 7: Different Versions of Blue Skies / Clouds
  • 8: Stormy
  • 9: Cloudy and Foggy
  • 10: Clear Blue Sky
  • 11: Scorching Hot (Los Santos heat waves)
  • 12 to 15: Very Dull, Colourless, Hazy
  • 16: Dull, Cloudy, Rainy
  • 17 to 18: Scorching Hot
  • 19: Sandstorm
  • 20: Foggy, Greenish
  • 21 to 22: Very dark, gradiented skyline, purple
  • 23 to 26: Pale orange
  • 27 to 29: Fresh blue
  • 30 to 32: Dark, cloudy, teal
  • 33: Dark, cloudy, brown
  • 34: Blue/purple, regular
  • 35: Dull brown
  • 36 to 38: Bright, foggy, orange
  • 39: Very bright
  • 40 to 42: Blue/purple cloudy
  • 43: Toxic clouds
  • 44: Black/white sky
  • 51 to 53: Amazing draw distance
  • 150: Darkest weather ever
  • 700: Stormy weather with pink sky and crystal water
